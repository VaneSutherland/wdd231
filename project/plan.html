Site Plan: Nourishing Recipes: Eat Well, Feel Great

Site Name

The site will be called Nourishing Recipes: Eat Well, Feel Great, aimed at providing a collection of gluten-free, sugar-free, and healthy recipes. This name was selected as it clearly reflects the goal of offering nutritious recipes suited to different dietary needs.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a platform that organizes healthy recipes by type (gluten-free, sugar-free, vegan, etc.). Nutritional information, including macros and calories, will be available upon visiting the recipe link, fetched directly from a free and accessible API.

Scenarios

  • What are the best keto-friendly recipes for those following a low-carb diet?
  • Where can I find nutritional information for each recipe (macros and calories) for vegan and low-carb options?
  • Are there easy vegan and gluten-free recipes suitable for a plant-based, low-carb lifestyle?

Color Scheme

The selected colors for this site are:

  • (Blue, #0c003f) - Used for subtitles and highlighted elements.
  • (Light blue, #c5dbe2) - Background color of the site.

Typography

The typography will use the Roboto.

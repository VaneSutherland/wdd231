Site Plan: Healthy Recipes

Site Name

The site will be called Healthy Recipes, aimed at providing a collection of gluten-free, sugar-free, and healthy recipes. This name was selected as it clearly reflects the goal of offering nutritious recipes suited to different dietary needs.

Optional available domain: healthyrecipes.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to offer a platform that groups healthy recipes categorized by type (gluten-free, sugar-free, vegan, etc.). Additionally, the site will display nutritional information such as macros and calories, fetched through a free and accessible API.

Scenarios

  • What are the best sugar-free recipes for people with diabetes?
  • Where can I find nutritional information for each recipe (macros and calories)?
  • Are there easy gluten-free recipes for people with gluten intolerance?

Color Scheme

The selected colors for this site are:

  • (Blue) - Used for main headings and buttons.
  • (Orange) - Used for subtitles and highlighted elements.
  • (Light Gray) - Background color of the site.

Typography

The typography will use the Arial font for main body text and Roboto for headings.
